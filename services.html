Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide services outside Ishwaramangala? +
Yes, we cover events across multiple nearby districts and states. Additional travel and logistics charges may apply based on the distance.
Do you offer customizable stage packages? +
Absolutely! Every event is unique. We tailor our stage, lighting, and seating designs to match your specific theme and budget requirements.
How far in advance should we book? +
We recommend booking at least 1-2 months in advance, especially during the peak wedding season, to ensure availability of our premium setups.

We provide all catering equipment, buffet setups, and dining ware. We do not provide the food itself, but we can recommend excellent local caterers!
